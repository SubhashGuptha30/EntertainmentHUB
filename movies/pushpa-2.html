Pushpa-2: The Rule

It is the second installment in the Pushpa film series and the sequel to Pushpa: The Rise. 10% of the film's footage was initially shot back-to-back with the original film. However, Sukumar decided to alter the story, which led to the film's principal photography commencing in October 2022.
