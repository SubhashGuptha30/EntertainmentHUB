One Punch Man


Anime Action superhero Comedy Parody Sci-Fi

One-punch-Man

"One Punch Man" is a Japanese anime series based on the webcomic created by ONE and its subsequent manga adaptation illustrated by Yusuke Murata. The story follows Saitama, an ordinary man who becomes a superhero for fun. He has trained so rigorously that he can defeat any opponent with a single punch, leading to an existential crisis as he grows bored of his overwhelming strength and the lack of challenging opponents.

Despite his immense power, Saitama remains an unrecognized hero within the Hero Association, often overshadowed by flashier and more charismatic heroes. Alongside his cyborg disciple, Genos, Saitama encounters a variety of foes, from monstrous creatures to rogue superhumans, all while seeking a worthy opponent who can give him a real fight.
