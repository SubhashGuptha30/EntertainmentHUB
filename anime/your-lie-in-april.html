Your Lie in April

"Your Lie in April" is a Japanese anime series based on the manga of the same name by Naoshi Arakawa. The story follows Kousei Arima, a piano prodigy who loses his ability to hear the sound of the piano after his abusive mother, who was also his instructor, passes away. This traumatic experience leads Kousei to abandon playing the piano, leaving him trapped in a monotonous and colorless life.

Kousei's world changes when he meets Kaori Miyazono, a spirited and free-spirited violinist. Kaori's passionate approach to music and her vibrant personality gradually reignite Kousei's love for the piano and life itself. As they perform together and grow closer, Kousei begins to confront his past traumas and embrace his future. However, Kaori harbors a secret that will impact both their lives profoundly.
