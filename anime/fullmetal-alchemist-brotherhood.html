Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood

"Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood" is a Japanese anime series that closely follows the original manga by Hiromu Arakawa. It is set in a world where alchemy is a widely practiced science and follows the journey of two brothers, Edward and Alphonse Elric. After a failed alchemical experiment to bring their mother back to life, Edward loses his left leg and right arm, while Alphonse loses his entire body, his soul being bound to a suit of armor. To restore their bodies, they seek the Philosopher's Stone, a powerful alchemical artifact.

Throughout their quest, they uncover deep conspiracies involving the military, homunculi (artificial humans), and the true nature of the Philosopher's Stone. The series explores themes of sacrifice, the consequences of ambition, and the bonds of family and friendship.
