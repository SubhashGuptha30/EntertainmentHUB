A Silent Voice

"A Silent Voice" is a Japanese animated drama film based on the manga of the same name by Yoshitoki Ōima. The story follows a young boy named Shoya Ishida, who bullies a deaf girl named Shoko Nishimiya during elementary school. As a result, Shoko transfers to another school, and Shoya becomes ostracized and bullied by his classmates. Years later, Shoya, now a high school student, is filled with guilt and seeks redemption by trying to make amends with Shoko. The film delves into themes of bullying, disability, forgiveness, and the quest for personal redemption.
