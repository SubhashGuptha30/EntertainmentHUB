Classroom of The Elite

"Classroom of the Elite" is set in the prestigious Tokyo Metropolitan Advanced Nurturing High School, which boasts a 100% employment and college entry rate for its students. The story follows Kiyotaka Ayanokoji, a seemingly average student who prefers to keep a low profile. The school separates students into four classes, A to D, based on merit, and provides them with luxurious facilities and a monthly allowance. However, students in lower-ranked classes face tougher conditions and fewer privileges.

Ayanokoji is placed in Class D, which is considered the worst class, filled with problem students. As he befriends the aloof Suzune Horikita and the cheerful Kikyo Kushida, he becomes involved in the school's complex system where students are pitted against each other in a battle of wits, strategy, and social manipulation. The series explores themes of competition, power dynamics, and the true nature of meritocracy.
