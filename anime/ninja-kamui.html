Ninja Kamui


Anime Action Sci-Fi Martial Arts Thriller

Ninja-Kamui

The story revolves around Joe Higan, a former ninja who has left his violent past behind and settled into a peaceful life in rural America. However, when his past catches up with him and his family is brutally murdered by his old clan, Joe is forced to take up his former identity as Kamui. Seeking revenge and justice, Kamui re-enters the shadowy world of ninjas, assassins, and mercenaries.

The series promises intense action, blending traditional ninja warfare with a futuristic setting. As Kamui battles through hordes of enemies and uncovers dark conspiracies, he must confront his inner demons and reconcile his dual identity as both a ruthless ninja and a grieving family man.
