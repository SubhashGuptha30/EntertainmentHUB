Jujutsu Kaisen 0

"Jujutsu Kaisen 0" is a Japanese animated film based on the manga series "Jujutsu Kaisen 0: Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical School" by Gege Akutami, which serves as a prequel to the main "Jujutsu Kaisen" series. The story centers on Yuta Okkotsu, a high school student plagued by the curse of his childhood friend, Rika Orimoto, who died in a tragic accident and transformed into a powerful, vengeful spirit that haunts him. Rika's curse causes harm to those around Yuta, leading him to live in isolation.

Yuta's life takes a turn when he meets Satoru Gojo, a teacher at the Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical School, who offers to help him control Rika's curse. Yuta enrolls in the school and learns to harness his own innate abilities to combat curses. Alongside his classmates, including Maki Zenin, Toge Inumaki, and Panda, Yuta embarks on a journey to break Rika's curse and confront dark, supernatural threats.
