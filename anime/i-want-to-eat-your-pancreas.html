I Want To Eat Your Pancreas

"I Want to Eat Your Pancreas" is a Japanese animated film based on the novel of the same name by Yoru Sumino. The story follows an introverted high school boy who discovers the diary of his classmate, Sakura Yamauchi, and learns that she is suffering from a terminal pancreatic illness. Despite their contrasting personalities—Sakura being cheerful and outgoing, while the protagonist is reserved and indifferent—they form an unlikely friendship. As they spend more time together, they grow closer, and the protagonist learns to embrace life and human connections.

The film explores themes of mortality, friendship, and the impact of relationships. It delves into how two people can change each other's lives profoundly even in a short amount of time.
